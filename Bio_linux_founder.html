Biography

Linus Torvalds is a Finnish-American software engineer who is the creator and principal developer of the Linux kernal,which is the kernal for a number of operating system.He also created the version controlsystem Git,Torvalds was born on December 28,1969 in Helsinki,Finland.He studied computer science at the University of Helsinki,and he developed the Linux kernal as a student project in 1991.Since then,the Linux kernal has becomeone of the most widely used kernals in the world.,powering everthing from smartphones to supercomputer.

  • 2004-Named one of the "100 Most Influential People in the World" by Time magzinein 2004
  • 2010-Recieved an honorary doctorate from the University of helsinki in 2010
  • 2010-Recieved the 2010 Milennium Technology Prize and the 2012 free Software Foundation's Award for the Advancement of the free Software
  • 2013-Inducted into the Internet Hall of Frame in 2013 for his contributions to the development of the Linux kernal and the creation of Git
  • 2014-Inducted into the Hall Frame of the Computer History Museum in 2014
  • 2015-Received the 2015 IEEE Compter Society Computer Pioneer Award
  • 2019-Received the 2019 ACM SIGOPS Hall of the Frame Award
  • 2021-Awarded the Takeda Foundation's 2021 Takeda Award for Social Contribution, which recognizes invdividual who have made significant contribution to the society through technology
  • 2021-Received the 2021 IEEE Masaru Ibuka Consumer Electronics Award ,which recognizes individual who significant contribution to the field consumer electronics
